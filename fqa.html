Frequently Asked Questions

What are your store hours?

Our store is open from 10 AM to 9 PM from Monday to Saturday, and from 12 PM to 6 PM on Sundays. During holidays, hours may vary, so please check our website or contact us directly for updated information.

Do you offer delivery or curbside pickup?

Yes, we offer both delivery and curbside pickup services. For delivery, you can place an order through our website or by phone. For curbside pickup, simply place your order online and call us when you arrive at the store. We will bring your order to your car.

Can I return or exchange a bottle if I don't like it?

We accept returns or exchanges for unopened bottles within 30 days of purchase with a valid receipt. Unfortunately, we cannot accept returns of opened bottles unless the product is defective. If you encounter any issues with your purchase, please contact us, and we will do our best to assist you.

Do you offer any discounts for bulk purchases or special events?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk purchases and special events such as weddings, parties, and corporate events. Please contact our store manager for details on our discount policies and to discuss your specific needs.

Do you carry rare or limited-edition wines and liquors?

Yes, we have a selection of rare and limited-edition wines and liquors. Our inventory changes frequently, so we recommend visiting our store or checking our website for the latest arrivals. If you are looking for something specific, please let us know, and we can assist in finding it for you.
